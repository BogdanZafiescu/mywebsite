About Me

Hi, I’m Bogdan — an IT Support & Digital Operations Specialist with a strong passion for technology and practical coding experience.
My interest in tech started back in high school, and now I’m fortunate to work in a role that keeps me engaged and constantly learning.

I’m based in Bucharest and currently work at Casa Schiller as an IT Support & Digital Operations Specialist. My responsibilities include maintaining desktop systems and peripherals, configuring software and operating systems, managing user accounts and backups, supporting local networks, and assisting with AV and email systems. I also manage the organization’s WordPress website, performing updates, front-end edits, and content optimization for mobile devices.

In addition to my IT support duties, I have practical coding experience in front-end web development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and Java programming. I’ve completed a few small Java projects focused on automating operating system tasks and basic gaming applications. These projects have helped me build problem-solving skills and deepen my understanding of programming concepts.

This website was designed and coded entirely by me, showcasing my hands-on skills in both development and design.

I am certified in NDG Linux Essentials, and I bring a balanced mix of technical support, coding, and problem-solving skills to my work. I enjoy creating practical solutions that improve workflows and user experiences.
